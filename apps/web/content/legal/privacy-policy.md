## Introduction

PilotSocials ("we", "our", or "us") operates the PilotSocials platform at app.pilotsocials.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- Name and email address (via Google OAuth)
- Organization/brand name you provide during setup

### Social Media Data
When you connect social media accounts, we access:
- Your social media profile information
- Post engagement metrics and analytics
- Content you create through our platform

We do NOT access your personal messages, private posts, or data from accounts you don't explicitly connect.

### Usage Data
We automatically collect:
- Log data (IP address, browser type, pages visited)
- Device information
- Usage patterns within the platform

### Payment Information
Payment processing is handled by Stripe. We do not store your credit card details. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data.

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the PilotSocials service
- Generate AI content based on your brand profile
- Schedule and publish content to your connected social accounts
- Analyze performance and provide analytics
- Send service-related communications
- Improve our platform and develop new features

## Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We share data only with:
- **Stripe** - for payment processing
- **Social media platforms** - to publish content you approve (Instagram, Facebook, TikTok)
- **AI service providers** - to generate content (data is processed, not stored by these providers)
- **Hosting providers** - for infrastructure (Railway, Neon)

## Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we remove your personal data within 30 days, except where required by law.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your account and data
- Export your data
- Opt out of marketing communications

## Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

## Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (HTTPS), secure authentication (OAuth 2.0), and encrypted database storage.

## Children's Privacy

PilotSocials is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification.
